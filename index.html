About Synapse

Learning that blends creativity, science, and engineering.

Synapse is a teaching center in Baalbeck focused on delivering practical STEM education through a maker-first approach. We prioritize real-world projects, critical thinking, and supportive mentoring that prepares learners for future technologies.

Project-first learning

Students build IoT devices, robots, and printed prototypes from day one to master concepts quickly.

Small group mentoring

Every cohort has a low student-to-teacher ratio, ensuring focused guidance and feedback.

Future-ready skills

Courses blend modern programming tools with physics and math fundamentals for long-term success.
